Our Story

Where Every Cup
Tells a Story

Founded in 2020, Cozy Coffee Shop started as a small dream to bring the finest coffee experience to Vijayawada. We source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them to perfection.

Our cozy space is designed for work, meetings, or simply unwinding with a good book and your favorite brew.

Premium Arabica beans sourced from Ethiopia and Colombia
Artisanal pastries baked fresh daily
Free WiFi and comfortable seating for all
